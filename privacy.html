Privacy Policy

Macro-Fit · Effective June 4th, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Carter Scott ("we," "us," "our") handles information in the Macro-Fit mobile app (the "App"). We built Macro-Fit to be privacy-respecting: we do not use any third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking software, and we do not sell your personal information.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information. When you create an account, we collect your email address and a securely hashed password (or, if you choose Sign in with Google or Apple, the basic profile information those providers send us, such as your name and email). You may also set a display name.
  • Food and nutrition data you enter. The foods, meals, portions, and goals you log, including any custom foods or saved meals you create and notes you add. This is the core data that powers the App's tracking, history, and trends.
  • Camera and photos. If you use barcode scanning, nutrition-label scanning, or recipe scanning, the App uses your camera or selected photos on your device only. Images are processed locally to read text or barcodes; images are never uploaded to or stored on our servers.
  • Subscription status. If you purchase a subscription, our payment partner tells us whether your subscription is active. We never receive or store your full payment card details.

2. How your searches and scans are used

When you search for a food or scan a barcode, the App sends the search text or barcode number to third-party food databases — USDA FoodData Central and Open Food Facts — to retrieve nutrition information. These requests are for looking up food data and do not include your account identity.

3. How we use your information

  • To provide the App's core features — logging, history, trends, saved meals, and goals.
  • To create and secure your account and keep you signed in.
  • To process and validate your subscription, if you purchase one.
  • To respond to your support requests.

We do not use your information for advertising, and we do not sell or rent your personal information to anyone.

4. Service providers we use

We rely on a small number of trusted providers that process data on our behalf:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and hosting for your account and logs (hosted in the United States).
  • USDA FoodData Central and Open Food Facts — food and nutrition lookups.
  • Apple and Google — optional Sign in with Apple / Google, and subscription billing through the App Store and Google Play.
  • RevenueCat — to validate and manage subscription status (used only if you subscribe).

5. Data retention and deletion

We keep your account and food-log history to provide the App's history and trends features; older detailed entries may be condensed into summaries over time. You can delete your account at any time from within the App (Profile → Delete Account), which removes your account and associated personal data from our systems. You may also contact us to request deletion.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal information, and to object to or restrict certain processing. You can exercise the core of these rights directly in the App, or by contacting us at the address below. We will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures — including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and access controls — to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your data and limit access to it.

8. Children's privacy

Macro-Fit is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. International users

We operate the App and store data in the United States. If you use the App from outside the United States, you understand that your information will be processed in the United States.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will revise the "Effective" date above and, for material changes, provide notice in the App.
